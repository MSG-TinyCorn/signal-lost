HOW TO PLAY

YOUR MISSION
The reactor is overloading. Power surges fly down 4 conduits. Press the matching key as a surge hits the glowing ring to reroute it.
THE KEYS
4 conduits — 4 keys: A S J K. Left hand covers A and S. Right hand covers J and K. Hit the key matching the conduit the surge is in.
MISSING
Miss a surge and it hits the reactor — you lose a life. Lose all lives and the station explodes. Use the Continue button to keep going!
SURGE TYPES
Purple = normal (15pts) · Cyan diamond = bonus (30pts) · Purple ghost = 40pts but flickers — time it carefully! Appears from level 3.
COINS
Earn coins by clearing sectors. Buy more coins cheaply from the shop. Spend coins on surge skins in the Skins shop!
STREAK
Hit 5 in a row for a x1.5 score bonus. Missing resets your streak to zero. Keep it going for massive points!
